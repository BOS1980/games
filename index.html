How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle:

  • W or Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S or Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • A/D or Left/Right Arrows: Steer
  • Space: Handbrake (for initiating drifts)

Master the perfect timing of throttle, brake, and steering to achieve the longest and most controlled drifts!

Become the Drift King

Drifting is an art form that requires precision, timing, and practice. In Leezkde, you'll learn to balance speed and control as you navigate challenging corners and hairpin turns.

Start with the basics: approach a corner at moderate speed, turn into the corner while tapping the handbrake, then counter-steer and modulate the throttle to maintain the perfect drift angle. With practice, you'll chain together spectacular drifts that earn you points and the admiration of other racers.

Challenge yourself on increasingly difficult tracks, unlock faster cars, and climb the global leaderboards to prove you're the ultimate Drift King!
